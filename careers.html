Join Our Creative Team

At Brightstar Animation, we're always looking for talented individuals who are passionate about bringing stories to life through animation. Join our team and be part of creating magical experiences!

Lead Animator

Create and oversee the animation of characters, objects, and environments. Work closely with other animators to maintain consistency and quality.

Requirements:

  • 5+ years of animation experience
  • Proficiency in industry-standard animation software
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating character animation
  • Experience in both 2D and 3D animation

Character Artist

Design and create compelling characters that capture hearts and imagination. Develop character models, textures, and rigging setups.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of character design experience
  • Strong understanding of anatomy and character design principles
  • Experience with digital sculpting and texturing
  • Knowledge of rigging fundamentals

Voice Actor

Bring characters to life through voice performance. Work with our creative team to develop unique character voices and personalities.

Requirements:

  • Professional voice acting experience
  • Ability to perform various voice styles and accents
  • Strong improvisational skills
  • Home recording setup preferred

Storyboard Artist

Create visual narratives that guide our animation process. Translate scripts into compelling sequential art.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of storyboarding experience
  • Strong drawing and compositional skills
  • Understanding of camera angles and cinematography
  • Ability to work with tight deadlines

How to Apply

Send your resume, portfolio, and cover letter to careers@brightstaranimation.com

We review applications on a rolling basis and will contact qualified candidates for interviews.
