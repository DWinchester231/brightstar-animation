About Us

BrightStar Animation was founded in 2024 by Donald Winchester, a passionate 20-year-old animation advocate who lives and breathes animation. Through determination and a vision for creating magical content, Donald's journey led him to Backstage, where he discovered two extraordinary talents: Kayda Cruz, a 15-year-old animation prodigy, and Eden Szijjarto, our brilliant assistant director.

Together, this dynamic trio forms the heart of BrightStar Animation, united by a shared mission: to create the most enchanting children's musical animated series that families can enjoy together. Our young and innovative team brings fresh perspectives, boundless creativity, and unwavering dedication to every project.
