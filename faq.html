Frequently Asked Questions

What types of animation services do you offer?
We specialize in 2D animation for children's content, including character animation, storyboarding, and complete animated series production. Our services cover concept development, character design, animation production, and post-production work.
How long does an animation project typically take?
Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A short 1-2 minute animation might take 4-6 weeks, while a full episode could take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.
What is your pricing structure?
Our pricing is project-based and depends on factors like animation complexity, length, character count, and timeline. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your project requirements. Contact us for a personalized estimate.
Do you offer revisions in your animation projects?
Yes, we include a set number of revision rounds in our project quotes. We believe in getting your feedback at key stages of production to ensure the final product matches your vision perfectly.
Who owns the rights to the animated content?
Upon full payment, you receive complete ownership rights to the final animated content. We retain the right to showcase the work in our portfolio unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.
Can you help with script writing and story development?
Yes! We offer comprehensive pre-production services including script writing, storyboarding, and character development. Our team can help develop your idea from concept to final animation.
What format will the final animation be delivered in?
We deliver animations in industry-standard formats (MP4, MOV) optimized for your intended platform (web, TV, social media). We can also provide the raw project files upon request.
Do you work with clients internationally?
Yes, we work with clients worldwide! We use video conferencing and project management tools to maintain clear communication across time zones.
